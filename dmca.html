Copyright Complaints

If you believe content is being accessed through ReelAGP in a way that infringes your rights, send a removal request with enough information for us to review it.

Include These Details

  • Your name and contact information.
  • The Instagram URL or page involved.
  • A description of the copyrighted work.
  • A statement that you are the owner or authorized to act for the owner.

Review

After receiving a valid request, we may block access, remove references, or take other reasonable action.
