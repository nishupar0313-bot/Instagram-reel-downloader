Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

ReelAGP is a public Instagram media downloader tool. This page explains what information may be collected when you use the website.

Information We Process

When you submit a public Instagram URL, the link is sent to our server so the requested media can be resolved. We do not ask for your Instagram password and you should never enter private account credentials on this website.

Logs And Analytics

Our hosting provider may keep standard server logs such as IP address, browser type, request time, and visited pages. These logs are used for security, debugging, and abuse prevention.

Advertising

We may show ads through third-party advertising partners such as Google AdSense. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads.

Cookies

ReelAGP itself does not require account cookies. Third-party services, including ads or analytics providers, may set cookies according to their own policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the contact page.
